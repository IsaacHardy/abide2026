ABIDE 2026

“If you abide in me, and my words abide in you, ask whatever you wish, and it will be done for you.”

John 15:7 ESV

Bring Your People, Let’s Abide Together!

ABIDE 2026 is a two-day gathering for women who are ready to go deeper in their faith. Set in the heart of Macon, Georgia, this conference brings together dynamic speakers, worship leaders, and a community of women who believe the Word of God is living, active, and transformative.

Whether you come with your small group, your best friend, or on your own, ABIDE is the space where you can exhale, be encouraged, and be reminded that you were made for more. Expect powerful teaching, bold worship, and genuine connection.

SPEAKERS

Speaker Lineup

Hosanna Wong

Hosanna Wong is an internationally known speaker, author, and spoken word artist who grew up in inner-city San Francisco. With raw honesty and deep biblical conviction, she inspires audiences to live boldly in their faith. Her passion is to equip this generation with the truth of Scripture.

Chrystal Evans Hurst

Chrystal Evans Hurst is a bestselling author, speaker, and the eldest daughter of Dr. Tony Evans. With warmth and wisdom, she encourages women to pursue God with all their hearts while navigating the beautiful mess of everyday life. She is a voice of grace and authenticity.

Annie F. Downs

Annie F. Downs is a New York Times bestselling author and host of the wildly popular podcast "That Sounds Fun." Her infectious joy and depth of faith make her one of the most relatable voices in Christian culture. She believes God is good and that life should be enjoyed.

Courtney Smallbone

Courtney Smallbone is an author, speaker, and the wife of Luke Smallbone of the Grammy-winning duo for KING & COUNTRY. Her story of faith through loss and redemption encourages women to trust God through every chapter. She speaks with vulnerability and unwavering hope.

Natalie Runion

Natalie Runion is an author, speaker, and ministry leader whose heart beats for women who feel unseen. She challenges audiences to lay down the burden of striving and step into the freedom of God's calling. Her message is deeply rooted in identity and purpose.

Jess Connolly

Jess Connolly is a bestselling author, speaker, and church planter from Charleston, South Carolina. She is passionate about calling women into their God-given authority and reminding them they are equipped for the mission. Her energy and conviction are absolutely contagious.

WORSHIP

Worship

Leeland

Leeland is a Grammy-nominated worship artist whose music has become a staple in churches worldwide. Known for songs like "Way Maker" and "Inhabit," his worship leads people into a deep, transformative encounter with God. His passion on stage is undeniable.

Hope Darst

Hope Darst is a worship leader and songwriter best known for her powerful anthem "Peace Be Still." With a voice that carries both strength and tenderness, she creates space for healing and hope in every room she enters. Her heart is to see people encounter the living God.

Frequently Asked Questions

ABIDE 2026 will be held in Macon, Georgia. The specific venue details, including the full address and parking instructions, will be shared with ticket holders closer to the event date. Macon is centrally located and easily accessible from most major cities in the Southeast.

Your ticket includes access to all conference sessions on both Friday evening and Saturday, including all speaker sessions and worship experiences. Meals are not included, but there will be a designated lunch break on Saturday with nearby dining options. A conference guide and materials will also be provided.

Yes, parking will be available at the venue. Detailed parking information, including lot locations and any associated fees, will be communicated to registered attendees prior to the event. We recommend arriving early on both days to secure a convenient spot.

Absolutely! Macon has a range of hotel options at various price points. We will be releasing a curated list of recommended hotels, including properties with special conference rates, closer to the event. We encourage out-of-town guests to book early for the best availability.

Tickets are non-refundable but are transferable. If you are unable to attend, you may transfer your ticket to another person by contacting our team at least 14 days before the event. Please reach out to us directly for transfer instructions and any questions about the policy.

Childcare is not provided at the venue. ABIDE is designed as a time for women to step away and be refreshed, so we encourage attendees to make arrangements for childcare during the conference. We want you to be fully present and able to receive all that the weekend has to offer.
