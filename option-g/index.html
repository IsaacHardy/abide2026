What is ABIDE?

ABIDE is a two-day women's conference created by Living Truth Collective, rooted in the conviction that God's Word is alive, active, and transforming. We gather women from every walk of life to sit at the feet of Jesus, learn what it means to remain in Him, and be sent out with renewed purpose and courage.

This is not just an event. It is an invitation to pause, to be nourished by truth, and to experience the kind of community that reminds you that you are not alone in your faith. Whether you bring your small group, your best friend, or come on your own, ABIDE is a place where you belong.

Abide in me, and I in you. As the branch cannot bear fruit by itself, unless it abides in the vine, neither can you, unless you abide in me.
John 15:4 (ESV)

Our Speaker Lineup

Six women whose lives and messages will challenge, comfort, and inspire you.

Hosanna Wong

Hosanna Wong is a speaker, author, and spoken word artist who travels internationally sharing the gospel. Her passion is helping people understand their identity in Christ and live with bold, unshakable faith. She has authored several books and reaches millions through her dynamic ministry.

Chrystal Evans Hurst

Chrystal Evans Hurst is a bestselling author, speaker, and the eldest daughter of Dr. Tony Evans. Her ministry encourages women to pursue God wholeheartedly while navigating real-life challenges. She is known for her warmth, vulnerability, and practical wisdom rooted in Scripture.

Annie F. Downs

Annie F. Downs is a New York Times bestselling author and host of the popular "That Sounds Fun" podcast. Her joyful spirit and honest storytelling have made her a beloved voice in the Christian community. She is passionate about helping people find the fun in faith and everyday life.

Courtney Smallbone

Courtney Smallbone is an author, speaker, and wife of Luke Smallbone of the Grammy-winning duo for KING & COUNTRY. Her ministry centers on encouraging women to find beauty and purpose in every season of life. She shares openly about faith, family, and the transforming power of God's love.

Natalie Runion

Natalie Runion is an author, speaker, and ministry leader whose work focuses on helping women release the pressure to be perfect and embrace God's grace. She leads with authenticity and compassion, guiding others to find rest and purpose in Christ. Her books and speaking engagements inspire women across the country.

Jess Connolly

Jess Connolly is a bestselling author, speaker, and church planter based in Charleston, SC. She is known for her energetic and Spirit-led teaching that calls women to walk boldly in their God-given purpose. Through her books and ministry, she empowers women to break free from comparison and live fully alive in Christ.

Expect powerful teaching, bold worship, and genuine connection.
