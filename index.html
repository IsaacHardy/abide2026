Frequently Asked Questions

ABIDE 2026 will be held in Macon, Georgia. The specific venue details and address will be shared with ticket holders closer to the event date. Macon is conveniently located in central Georgia with easy access from major highways and the Middle Georgia Regional Airport.

Your ticket includes access to all conference sessions on Friday evening and Saturday, as well as worship experiences with our featured artists. Please note that meals and lodging are not included in the ticket price but we have recommendations for nearby dining and hotels.

Yes, free parking is available at the venue. There is ample space for all attendees. Additional parking details and maps will be sent to ticket holders in advance of the event.

Absolutely! We will provide a list of recommended hotels near the venue with special group rates for conference attendees. We encourage you to book early as rooms tend to fill up quickly.

Tickets are refundable up to 30 days before the event. After that date, tickets are non-refundable but may be transferred to another attendee. Please contact our team for transfer requests.

Childcare is not provided at the venue during the conference. We encourage attendees to make arrangements in advance. This is a wonderful opportunity to invest in your own spiritual growth.
