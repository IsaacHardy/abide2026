Our Mission

A Weekend to Root Deeper

ABIDE 2026 is a gathering designed for women who want to move beyond surface-level faith. Over two days, you will be immersed in powerful teaching, heartfelt worship, and authentic community.

Our hope is that you leave not just inspired, but transformed -- deeply rooted in the love of Christ and equipped to carry that connection into every corner of your life.

Speakers

Meet the Voices

Speaker

Hosanna Wong

Hosanna Wong is a speaker, author, and spoken word artist who travels internationally sharing the gospel. Her passion is helping people understand their identity in Christ and walk in the freedom and calling that God has for them. With a unique blend of artistry and biblical truth, Hosanna reaches audiences of all ages, inspiring them to live boldly and authentically for Jesus.

Speaker

Chrystal Evans Hurst

Chrystal Evans Hurst is a bestselling author, speaker, and the eldest daughter of Dr. Tony Evans. She encourages women to pursue God wholeheartedly, sharing her own experiences of balancing faith, family, and purpose with warmth and vulnerability. Chrystal is known for helping women find the beauty in their own unique stories while drawing closer to the heart of God.

Speaker

Annie F. Downs

Annie F. Downs is a New York Times bestselling author and the host of the beloved "That Sounds Fun" podcast. Known for her joyful spirit and honest storytelling, Annie invites women into deeper conversations about faith, courage, and finding delight in everyday life. Her relatable voice and contagious enthusiasm make her a favorite among women seeking authentic community and spiritual growth.

Speaker

Courtney Smallbone

Courtney Smallbone is an author and speaker whose ministry centers on finding beauty in every season of life. As the wife of Luke Smallbone of the Grammy-winning duo for KING & COUNTRY, Courtney brings a unique perspective on faith, family, and perseverance. Her gentle yet powerful voice encourages women to trust God's timing and find purpose in both mountaintop and valley moments.

Speaker

Natalie Runion

Natalie Runion is an author, speaker, and ministry leader who helps women release the pressure to be perfect and instead embrace the freedom found in Christ. With honesty and grace, Natalie addresses the struggles of comparison, burnout, and striving, offering a path toward peace and purpose. Her message resonates with women who long to live authentically and rest in God's sufficiency.

Speaker

Jess Connolly

Jess Connolly is a bestselling author, church planter, and speaker from Charleston, South Carolina, who calls women to walk boldly in their God-given purpose. With a fierce love for the local church and a passion for equipping women, Jess combines theological depth with refreshing vulnerability. Her words challenge women to stop playing small and start living out the abundant life God has designed for them.
