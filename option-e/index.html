Welcome to ABIDE

ABIDE 2026 is a two-day women's conference designed to draw you closer to Christ through powerful worship, bold teaching, and genuine community. Whether you come alone or with friends, you'll find a space to rest, be challenged, and be renewed.

Abide in me, and I in you. As the branch cannot bear fruit by itself, unless it abides in the vine, neither can you, unless you abide in me.

John 15:4

Speakers

Six incredible women who will challenge, encourage, and inspire you.

Hosanna Wong

Speaker & Spoken Word Artist

Hosanna Wong is a speaker, author, and spoken word artist who has been touring globally since the age of 15. With a heart for revival and reaching every generation, she brings bold and creative messages that illuminate the truth of Jesus. Hosanna is the author of multiple books and is known for making God's Word come alive for audiences of all ages.

Chrystal Evans Hurst

Bestselling Author & Speaker

Chrystal Evans Hurst is a bestselling author, speaker, and worship leader. As the eldest daughter of Dr. Tony Evans, she draws on a rich legacy of faith while sharing her own authentic journey. Her warm and engaging style has made her a beloved voice in women's ministry, inspiring thousands to pursue deeper lives of faith and purpose.

Annie F. Downs

NYT Bestselling Author

Annie F. Downs is a New York Times bestselling author, sought-after speaker, and host of the popular "That Sounds Fun" podcast. Based in Nashville, Annie uses her gift of writing and speaking to communicate to audiences across the country. Known for her relatable and joyful approach to faith, she encourages women to embrace the life God has for them.

Courtney Smallbone

Author & Speaker

Courtney Smallbone is an author and speaker known for her warmth and authenticity. As the wife of Luke Smallbone of the Grammy-winning duo for KING & COUNTRY, Courtney brings a unique perspective on faith, family, and living with purpose. Her messages about identity and God's faithfulness resonate deeply with women seeking encouragement in every season of life.

Natalie Runion

Author & Ministry Leader

Natalie Runion is an author, speaker, and ministry leader with a passion for helping women discover their God-given calling. Through her writing and speaking, Natalie addresses the real challenges women face while pointing them to the hope found in Christ. Her authentic and grounded approach makes her a trusted voice in women's ministry.

Jess Connolly

Bestselling Author & Church Planter

Jess Connolly is a bestselling author, speaker, and church planter based in Charleston, South Carolina. She is the founder of All Good Things Collective and co-author of several books that have impacted women nationwide. Jess is known for her bold and compassionate approach to faith, empowering women to live with holy confidence and embrace the fullness of God's calling.

Frequently Asked Questions

ABIDE 2026 will be held in Macon, Georgia. The specific venue details will be shared with ticket holders closer to the event date. Stay tuned to our social media channels for updates.

Your ticket includes admission to all conference sessions on Friday evening and Saturday, access to all speakers and worship experiences, and a conference welcome gift. Meals are not included, but there will be a lunch break on Saturday with nearby dining options.

Yes, parking is available at the venue. Detailed parking information and directions will be provided to ticket holders prior to the event. We recommend arriving early to allow time for parking and check-in.

We are working on securing group hotel rates near the venue for ABIDE 2026 attendees. Hotel recommendation details will be shared on our website and social media once finalized. We recommend booking early as summer is a popular travel season in Macon.

Tickets are purchased through Ticketmaster and are subject to their refund policies. Please review Ticketmaster's terms and conditions at the time of purchase for details on cancellations and refunds. If you have specific concerns, feel free to reach out to us on social media.

Childcare is not provided at the conference venue. ABIDE 2026 is designed as a time for women to step away and be refreshed, so we encourage attendees to make arrangements for their children prior to attending. We want you to be fully present and enjoy every moment.
